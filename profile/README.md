*Claude Code plugin specialized in fullstack TOTVS Protheus development — for developers and functional analysts.*

## Sobre | About

A **AdvFlow** nasceu da experiência real de profissionais Protheus — desenvolvedores e analistas funcionais — que buscam produtividade sem abrir mão da qualidade. Combinamos conhecimento profundo do ecossistema TOTVS (ADVPL, TLPP, PO UI, processos de negócio) com o poder da IA para transformar o dia a dia de quem trabalha com o ERP líder da América Latina.

*AdvFlow was born from the real-world experience of Protheus professionals — developers and functional analysts — who seek productivity without compromising quality. We combine deep knowledge of the TOTVS ecosystem (ADVPL, TLPP, PO UI, business processes) with the power of AI to transform the daily workflow of those working with Latin America's leading ERP.*
